About JP Materials

Trade-quality rendering products, delivered direct to you.

Who We Are

JP Materials is a direct-to-consumer supplier of professional rendering products. We work with the same manufacturers used by the trade, giving homeowners and contractors access to premium materials without the premium markup.

Based in the UK, we're passionate about making high-quality rendering accessible — whether you're a DIY enthusiast tackling a first patch repair or a seasoned plasterer managing a full new-build specification.

What We Sell

Our range covers everything you need for a complete rendering job from scratch:

  • Base coats & bonding agents
  • Sand & cement mixes
  • Fine finish and textured renders
  • Acrylic and flexible renders
  • Coloured top coats
  • Waterproofing additives

Why Buy Direct?

Traditional wholesale channels mean multiple markups before the product reaches you. By selling direct, we pass those savings on — so you get the same materials at a fraction of the cost, with free delivery on every order.
